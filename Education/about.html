Our Story

Founded in 2018, EduPlatform started with a simple idea: to democratize education and make high-quality learning accessible to everyone. What began as a small team of passionate educators has grown into a global platform serving over 50,000 students worldwide.
Our platform has evolved from offering just a few courses to hosting over 1,200 comprehensive programs across multiple disciplines, all taught by industry experts and seasoned professionals.
