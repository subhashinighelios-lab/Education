Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our courses, pricing, certifications, and learning experience.

What types of courses are available on EduVate? +

EduVate offers courses in programming, data science, UI/UX design, business management, digital marketing, and career-oriented skills.

Do I receive a certificate after course completion? +

Yes, every completed course includes a verified digital certificate that can be shared on resumes and LinkedIn profiles.

Can I access courses on mobile and tablet devices? +

Absolutely. EduVate is fully responsive and optimized for mobile, tablet, and desktop learning.

Are beginner-friendly courses available? +

Yes. Many courses are designed specifically for beginners, requiring no prior technical knowledge.

How long will I have access to enrolled courses? +

You will get lifetime access to all enrolled courses, including future updates and additional resources.

Do you provide career or placement assistance? +

Selected plans include resume building, mock interviews, career guidance, and job placement support.

Can I switch or upgrade my learning plan later? +

Yes, you can upgrade or change your subscription plan at any time directly from your dashboard.

Is there any refund policy available? +

We offer a flexible refund policy within the trial period. Please refer to our refund guidelines for more details.

Are live classes or mentor sessions included? +

Premium plans include live mentor sessions, doubt-clearing classes, and one-on-one guidance.

How can I contact EduVate support? +

You can reach our support team through the contact page, email support, or live chat available on the platform.
